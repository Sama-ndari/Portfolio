E-Sama: Boutique & POS Management

A professional Flutter mobile application for shop and point-of-sale management, built for merchants in Burundi and East Africa. E-Sama supports 3 distinct store types (Retail, Restaurant, Rental), works fully offline, and is monetized through a subscription model with real paying users.

3 Store Types:

  • Vente (Retail) - Full POS with barcode scanning, product variants, wholesale pricing, multi-payment (Cash + Mobile Money + Credit), and parked sales
  • Restaurant - Menu management with categories (Beverage, Breakfast, Main Course, Dessert), ingredient tracking, and order flow
  • Location (Rental) - Equipment rental with duration picker (hourly/daily/weekly/monthly), caution deposits, overdue tracking, and return management

Key Features:

  • Offline-First Architecture - Hive local database with AES-256 encryption and secure storage
  • Smart Notifications - Low stock alerts, daily sales summary, debt reminders, recurring expense alerts, and subscription management
  • CRM & Loyalty - Client management with VIP tracking, WhatsApp debt reminders, and configurable loyalty points
  • Reports & Export - Sales, expenses, and inventory reports with PDF/Excel export, pie charts, and top-selling product analytics
  • Bluetooth Printing - ESC/POS thermal printer support for professional receipts and invoices
  • Remote Config - GitHub Gist-based configuration for app updates, kill switch, pricing, and admin messages

Subscription Tiers:

  • Standard - 1 store, basic POS & inventory
  • Business - 3 stores, PDF export, WhatsApp reminders, product variants
  • Premium - Unlimited stores, advanced AI features, all analytics
